### Action Item Categories

- **Body Composition:** Adjust nutrition, training volume, or cardio
- **Strength Progression:** Adjust program, increase/decrease volume, change exercises
- **Goal Trajectory:** Revise targets, adjust timeline, or shift priorities
- **System Performance:** Modify agent prompts, update preferences, adjust structure
